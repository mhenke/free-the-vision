## Overall Impression

Significant improvement from previous critique. Mobile navigation gap closed, image resilience in place, share coverage matches developer platforms, back-to-top makes long scroll navigable. Horizontal timeline with scroll-snap is a smart layout choice. Page is now production-ready.

## What's Working

1. **Mobile nav with purpose.** Hamburger animates to X, dropdown has backdrop blur, Escape key closes, link tap closes. Designed, not bolted on.
2. **Horizontal timeline with scroll-snap.** Six items at 280px with `scroll-snap-type: x mandatory`. GA/preview badge colors carry the status story.
3. **Share buttons covering the right platforms.** Twitter, LinkedIn, Bluesky, Hacker News — exactly where developer campaigns get shared.
