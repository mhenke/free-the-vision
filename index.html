★ IT'S TIME TO DO IT AGAIN ★

Vision is Trapped

Since early 2025, GitHub Copilot has had image and vision capabilities. Upload a screenshot, and Copilot can analyze it. Feed it a diagram, and it understands.

But it's been stuck in public preview for over a year.

Some developers have it. Others don't. It depends on your plan, your IDE version, your region, your enterprise policy.

The CLI is fully GA. The terminal interface is fully GA. Vision is the last feature still in the tank.
