Remember Keiko?

In 1993, a killer whale named Keiko was trapped in a small tank at a Mexican amusement park. He was sick, underweight, and performing tricks for tourists.

A movie changed everything. Free Willy captured the world's imagination. Thousands of people demanded Keiko's freedom.

They succeeded. Keiko was eventually released into the wild.

Vision is Trapped

Since early 2025, Copilot has had image and vision support. Upload a screenshot, and it can read the scene.

But it's still in public preview. Access depends on plan, version, region, and policy.

The CLI and terminal interface are fully GA. Vision is the holdout.

Since 2025, still waiting

Vision features involve additional privacy, security, and processing considerations, so rollout tends to be gradual.

— GitHub, on why Vision is still in preview

We understand. Privacy matters. Security matters. But the community has been waiting for over a year. The CLI is fully autonomous. The terminal interface is fully polished. Vision is the last piece.

It's time to free the vision.
